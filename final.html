A piggy bank that counts coins!


Concept -- a piggy bank that counts coins!


Demo Video





I used 4 pairs of IR break beam sensors for four slots I laser cut. They will detect
when the beam is broken and change the corresponding amount.
When you insert a coin, the coin will slide on a slide made of mat board with holes I
laser cut on it. Each hole is the same size as a coin. Under the hole, there is a pair
of IR Break Beam sensor. When the coin falls, it will break the beam and the sensor will
detect and update the amount of money. LCD will show the total amount of money.

I made the hole for insertion oblique because I want the slide inside to be oblique so that
coins can easily fall into the holes that are aligned on the side of the slide.
